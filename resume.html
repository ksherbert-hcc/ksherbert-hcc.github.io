Note to Students

This page gives my curriculum vitae, the course of my life, academia's alternative to a resume which is painfully comprehensive. By contrast, your resume should be a concise summary of your skills. The sections on this page which start expanded are good models for the scope and depth of content on a resume, but you should be creative with formatting to bring out your most meaningful experiences.

Remember that for every job you apply to, you should learn everything you can about the employer and then craft a new version of your resume just for them. But a generic resume on your personal website is a great backup to share in chance encounters.
